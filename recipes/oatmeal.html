Overnight Oats

Oatmeal is nutritionally rich

It has more protein than most grains and also contains numerous vitamins and minerals. It contains antioxidants and a soluble fiber called beta-glucan, which aids several systems of the body.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Mason Jar
  • Liquid
  • Honey
  • Nut Butter
  • Nuts
  • Fruits

Steps

  1. 1/2 cup of oats in mason jar
  2. 1/2 cup of liquid of your choice
  3. add fruit
  4. honey
  5. protein of your choice
  6. shake
  7. refrigerate
  8. enjoy
